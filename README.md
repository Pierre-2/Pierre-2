## 📊 Featured Projects

### 🎓 [Socioeconomic Drivers of Household Electricity Access in Rwanda](https://github.com/Pierre-2/RwandaElectricityAccess)
> BSc Thesis | University of Rwanda | 2026

- **Dataset:** EICV 7 (2023/24) — 15,054 households across 30 districts
- **Method:** Binary Logistic Regression + Probit + LPM + GIS Spatial Analysis
- **Key Finding:** University education increases electricity access probability by +34.8pp
- **Model Fit:** AUC-ROC = 0.811 | McFadden R² = 0.2271
- **Tools:** Python, LaTeX, GeoPandas, Statsmodels
